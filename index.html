    Audi R8: the Audi R8 is a mid-engine, two-seater
    supercar that combines luxury with high-performance. It features a
    striking design, advanced technology, and a powerful V10 engine
    option. The R8 is renowned for its exceptional handling and agility
    on the road and track.

    Audi RS7: The Audi RS7 is a high-performance version of
    the Audi A7, equipped with a V8 engine. It offers a perfect blend of
    sportiness and practicality, with a spacious interior and advanced
    technology. The RS7 is celebrated for its lightning-fast
    acceleration and sophisticated all-wheel-drive system.

    Audi TT RS: The Audi TT RS is a compact sports car that
    packs a powerful punch with its turbocharged five-cylinder engine.
    It delivers thrilling performance while maintaining the iconic TT
    design. The TT RS is known for its sharp handling and exhilarating
    driving experience.

    Audi Q8: While not a traditional supercar, the Audi Q8
    is a luxury SUV with a sporty edge. The Q8 features a bold design,
    spacious interior, and a range of powerful engines. It offers
    impressive performance and advanced technology, making it a
    versatile choice for those seeking both luxury and speed.

    Audi e-tron GT: The Audi e-tron GT is an all-electric
    grand tourer that combines stunning aesthetics with powerful
    electric performance. It features dual electric motors that deliver
    exceptional acceleration and handling. With its luxurious interior,
    cutting-edge technology, and impressive range, the e-tron GT sets a
    new standard for electric supercars, making it an exciting option
    for environmentally conscious enthusiasts.
